My process
01
Discover
Deep user research, interviews, competitive analysis, and stakeholder alignment to understand the real problem space before touching a wireframe.
02
Define
Synthesising insights into clear problem statements, personas, journey maps, and design principles that guide every subsequent decision.
03
Design
Rapid wireframing to high-fidelity prototyping — iterating quickly, always grounded in user needs and business goals.
04
Deliver
Validated through usability testing, then handed off with thorough documentation, design tokens, and close developer collaboration.
